Projects

  • Basic CRM

    Why was Basic CRM made?

    It has been my experience that having a way to organize clients, prospects, and customer data is an essential part of sales. It allows for a sales person to keep their work organized and makes customers feel better when the sales person remembers information after an interaction. The Basic CRM was my way of making my own customer database system.

  • Call Counter

    Why was "Call Counter" made?

    I was having a hard time tracking calls working in a call center job and I need to create a tool that would solve this problem. I made a tool that would count the numbers of calls I would get in a day. The "Call Counter" is the main tool I use at work to track my progress.
